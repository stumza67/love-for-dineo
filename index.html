Dineo

Happy Valentine’s Day

Somewhere between all the noise of the world,
there’s you — steady, kind, unforgettable.
This little page exists for one reason only:
to remind you how deeply you are loved.

I don’t love you loudly — I love you surely.
In quiet moments. In distance. In choice.
If home were a person, it would be you.
If forever had a name, it would be Dineo.

— Always yours
